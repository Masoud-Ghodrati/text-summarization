Ana Ivanovic won her first title for two years in Linz, Austria on Sunday.
Former world No. 1 defeated Patty Schnyder 6-1 6-2 to win hard-court event for second time.
Serbian did not drop a set for the duration of the tournament.
The 22-year-old is now ranked 26 in the world after falling to a low of 65 this year.
