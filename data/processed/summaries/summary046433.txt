Hosts Angola and Mali draw 4-4 in opening match of 27th Africa Cup of Nations.
Mali trailed 4-0 with 16 minutes left until mounting an incredible comeback.
Seydou Keita with a double, Frederic Kanoute and Mustapha Yatabare score Mali goals.
Flavio Armado with a double and penalties from Gilberto and Manucho put Angola in command.
