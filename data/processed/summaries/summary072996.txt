World No. 2 Vera Zvonareva and third seed Kim Clijsters reach Australian Open semis.

Zvonareva of Russia also has a straight-sets quarterfinal victory over Czech Petra Kvitova.
Caroline Wozniacki of Denmark and China's Li Na to contest other women's semifinal.
